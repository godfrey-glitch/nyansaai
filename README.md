## What is Nyansa AI?

Nyansa AI is a free, mobile-first learning platform built specifically for Ghanaian students from Nursery/KG all the way to SHS 3. It covers the full **GES curriculum (2024–2026)** including new subjects like OWOP, Career Technology, and Creative Arts & Design (CAD).

---

## Features

- 🎯 **AI Quiz Engine** — Infinite questions across 20+ GES subjects, adaptive difficulty
- 🤖 **AI Tutor** — Ask anything in English, Twi, Fante, Ewe, Ga, Hausa, or Dagbani
- ✍️ **Essay Grader** — Instant AI feedback on student writing
- 🃏 **Flashcards** — AI-generated study decks for any subject
- 📅 **Study Plan** — Personalised WASSCE/BECE/NSAT prep schedules
- 🏆 **WASSCE/BECE Prep** — Exam-style questions with marking schemes
- 📡 **Live Classroom** — Real-time group quizzes for teachers
- 👩‍🏫 **Teacher Dashboard** — Manage students and assign quizzes
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Parent Portal** — Monitor your child's progress
- ⭐ **Kids Mode** — Nursery/KG learning with ABC, 123, and fun quizzes
- 🗺️ **Mastery Map** — Track strengths and weak areas
- 🔊 **Voice System** — Text-to-speech and speech input in Ghanaian languages
- 🌙 **Dark Mode** + Dyslexia-friendly font + Large text accessibility options

---

## GES Subjects Covered

**Core (All Levels):** Mathematics, English, Integrated Science, Social Studies, History of Ghana, RME, P.E., French, Twi, Computing/ICT

**Primary (B1–B6):** Our World Our People (OWOP)

**JHS New Subjects (CCP):** Career Technology, Creative Arts & Design (CAD)

**SHS Electives:** Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Geography, Government, Literature, Elective Maths, Financial Accounting, Business Management
